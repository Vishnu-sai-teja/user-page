# About Me PRD

## Title

Vishnu Nagabandi — Personal About Me Experience

## Problem Statement

Vishnu needs a dedicated About Me page that goes beyond a resume summary. The page must present him as a thoughtful, credible, and well-rounded professional by communicating his identity, background, strengths, work, and personality in a way that feels intentional and memorable. The challenge is not to place content on a page — it is to shape that content into a personal experience with clear structure, strong hierarchy, and enough personality to feel authentic.

## Goals

- Give Vishnu a credible and distinctive online presence that reflects both professional substance and personal identity
- Present information in a clear and engaging way that rewards both quick scanning and deeper exploration
- Make projects, experience, and strengths easy to understand for a first-time visitor
- Leave the visitor with a clear sense of who Vishnu is and what he brings to a collaboration or role
- Feel more like a living personal website than a static resume

## Non-Goals

- Replace or replicate a traditional resume document
- Serve as a portfolio with exhaustive case studies or full project documentation
- Function as a blog or content publishing platform
- Include dynamic backend features such as user accounts, form submissions, or real-time data

## Target Audience

- Recruiters and hiring managers evaluating Vishnu as a candidate
- Collaborators, peers, or community members exploring a potential working relationship
- Clients or stakeholders assessing professional credibility
- Anyone who has encountered Vishnu's work and wants to learn more

## User Needs

- Quickly understand who Vishnu is and what he currently does
- Get a clear sense of his background, interests, and areas of expertise within seconds of landing on the page
- Find specific highlights — projects, achievements, experience — without having to dig
- Understand what makes Vishnu differentiated or interesting as a professional
- Know how to continue the conversation or reach out

## Experience Principles

- **Clarity over completeness** — every section should earn its place; no filler content
- **Narrative over listing** — content should tell a coherent story, not read like a database of facts
- **Confidence without inflation** — present Vishnu's work accurately and compellingly without overstating
- **Scan-friendly and depth-rewarding** — structure that works for a 30-second skim and a 5-minute read
- **Personal without casual** — reflect personality and voice while staying professionally credible

## Scope Summary

The About Me experience covers a single-page or multi-section personal profile for Vishnu Sai Teja Nagabandi. It must include a strong introduction, a concise summary, an experience section, a skills and strengths section, a projects section, and a contact or next-steps section. Supporting content areas — such as personal principles, featured highlights, or recommendations — may be included where they add value. The final application must be deployed to GitHub Pages.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The page includes all six required content areas: introduction, summary, experience, skills, projects, and contact
- A first-time visitor can scan the page in under 30 seconds and come away with a clear understanding of who Vishnu is and what he does
- The page reflects personal voice and does not read like a generic template
- Projects are clearly described with enough context to understand their significance
- Contact information or next steps are visible and actionable
- The complete application is successfully deployed to GitHub Pages and publicly accessible
